Welcome to Volley Stats app

This application will help you to collect, store and analyze individual statistics of volleyball players.

Fatures

Easy statistics picking

Designed specially for mobile devices and tablets, Volley Stats is comfortable for using in game. Some of your friends, fans, coach assistant or reserve players can collect statistics when your team is playing.

Powerful analytics

Volley Stats has a set of statistical indicators of the effectiveness of players. Looking at the results of the match, you can find out what contribution each player made and how useful his actions were.

Saving match results

You can download your results in cloud. Further results can be viewed and compared.

Free to use

You can use Volley Stats as many as you like. If you have suggestions for improving Volley Stats, please contact us.

Ideal for amateur teams

With Volley Stats you will bring your team to a new level!

Getting started

Volley Stats is intuitive and very easy to use. Collect your team`s statistics in 5 easy steps:

  • 1

    Add players

    Go to Volley Stats menu on top left corner of the screen and add your players. Each player can be field or reserve and has his game role.

  • 2

    Start round

    When your team is ready, press "Start Round".

  • 3

    Note game actions

    Watch your team and mark player`s game actions on the statistic`s gathering screen. There are four actions and three gradations of quality for each action: good (green), normal (yellow) and bad (red). Usually "good" means that player brought a point to the team, "normal" - no point but the ball is in game, "bad" - player lost a point.

  • 4

    Note game rounds (optional)

    You can mark new rounds during the match. If you do so, you will get match statistics for each round.

  • 5

    Save results

    If you are logged in, you can save the results in your profile, if not - just send the results to the email.
